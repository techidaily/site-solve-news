### Revolutionize Your Document Processes with No-Code Technology

**No-Code Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)** streamlines access to that data and more importantly, your staff do not require any training to use it.

As our webinar guest Edd Berry from Manchester University NHS Foundation Trust says: _"We can put this (IDP solution) in the hands of our team and we are already up-scaling them. We can really enable them to grow further."_

Watch the webinar and learn:

* The benefits of no-code IDP
* How the solution works
* Our success story with NHS

### Agenda

* **What Is No-Code IDP?**
* **Benefits**
