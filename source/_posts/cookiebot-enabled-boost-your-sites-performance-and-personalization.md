## Rise above the Competition Through Personalization in Financial Services

The global pandemic created increased digital engagement in financial services. As a result, neobanks, challenger banks, and FinTechs increased pressure on incumbent financial institutions, in nearly every sector — consumer banking, fund transfer and payments, investment and wealth management, and brokerage services. To combat this threat, traditional financial institutions should focus on delivering personalized experiences to their customers.
